WELCOME TO EARTH TREK!

Earth Trek is an interactive 3D web application that provides easy access for all kinds of users to the data collected and compiled by NASA's Earth Observing System (EOS) program.

It allows to follow the orbits of the different satellites in real time (from their launch until now and even into the future) and to visualize the data collected by their instruments.

With this tool, we aim to encourage new generations to get involved in NASA's programs, from spreading the word to becoming active members -or even pioneers- of present and future developments.

Public engagement is key in the pursuit of aerospace exploration.
